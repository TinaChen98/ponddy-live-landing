2. My internet connection is unstable.
No worries, please take the following steps to resolve the issue:
  1. Close other websites and running applications
  2. Turn off video camera
  3. If you are using Zoom on iPad or other tablets, try to use PC instead
  4. Use another browser, e.g. Google Chrome
  5. Use a wired-network connection instead of Wi-Fi

If the above methods don‚Äôt resolve the issue, please try the steps below for troubleshooting:
  1. Joining a Zoom test meeting to test your internet connection
  2. Test if your internet speed is above 8 Mbps. If the speed is below 8 Mbps, your internet may be too slow: https://fast.com/

If the issue still exists, please check your internet connection according to Zoom Support:

If you are experiencing any issue(s) with latency, frozen screen, poor quality audio, or meeting getting disconnected while using a home or non-enterprise WiFi connection, try the following:
  1. Watch a video about WiFi connectivity
  2. Check your Internet bandwidth using an online speed test, such as nperf, Speedtest, or Comparitech
  3. Try to connect directly via Wired (if your internet router has wired ports)
  4. Try bringing your computer or mobile device closer to the WiFi router or access point in your home or office
  5. Upgrade your WiFi router firmware. Check your WiFi router vendor support site for firmware upgrade availability
  6. Use a WiFi extender such as Amped Wireless or Linksys to increase the distance and strength of your WiFi signal
  7. Use a higher-powered/long-range WiFi such as Amped Wireless router or adapter to increase the WiFi signal on your PC or Mac
  8. [Home] Use a DOCSIS 3.0 cable modem (if you have cable modem) to improve internet performance

If you are on corporate WiFi, try the following:
  1. Switch to a Wired connection
  2. Switch to a different WiFi hotspot if possible
  3. Check your Internet bandwidth using an online speed test, such as nperf, Speedtest or Comparitech
  4. Contact your corporate IT department to check your WiFi hotspot
  5. Turn off group HD in your Account Management if your WiFi bandwidth is insufficient
  6. Turn off your corporate VPN if it's not required for the meeting

System
1. System requirements
You can take the lessons on various devices, including Windows and Mac desktops/laptops, as well as Android and iOS mobile devices. Please see below requirements for each system:

Minimum system requirements
  • The latest Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge or Opera for desktop/laptop
  • A broadband connection with a minimum speed of 5Mbit or 800kbps

System requirements for PC
  • Platform: Windows 8.1 or higher with the latest updates installed
  • RAM: 4GB or more
  • Video: Graphics output capability
  • Sound: Sound output capability

System requirements for macOS
  • Platform: Mac OS X 10.12 or higher with the latest updates installed
  • RAM: 4GB or more
  • Video: Graphics output capability
  • Sound: Sound output capability

System requirements for mobile
  • iOS 13.0 and above
  • Android 6.0 and above
  • Mobile browsers: Safari or Chrome on iOS and Chrome on Android
